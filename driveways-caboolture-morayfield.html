Ask what finish suits me ↗
Best value

Plain concrete

Clean, practical and cost-effective. A strong option when you want a durable driveway without decorative upgrades.

Smooth finish

Trowel finish

Smooth and dense for covered or low-slip-risk areas. We will advise if broom or swirl is safer for your driveway slope.

Driveway staple

Broom finish

Durable, slip-resistant and budget-friendly. The go-to finish for practical Queensland driveways.

Textured grip

Swirl finish

Hand-worked circular texture with more character than plain concrete and useful grip for outdoor surfaces.

Premium kerb appeal

Exposed aggregate

Premium street appeal with natural stone texture, strong wet-weather grip and long-term visual depth.

Colour upgrade

Coloured concrete

Adds warmth or contrast without going fully decorative. Works with broom, swirl and other surface textures.

Decorative pattern

Stamped concrete

Patterned concrete that can mimic stone, cobble, slate or brick for a feature driveway or entry.

Resurface option

Covercrete

For suitable existing concrete, resurfacing can refresh the look with colour and stencil options without a full rip-out.

Driveway quote questions

Good to know before we quote.

How much does a concrete driveway cost in Caboolture or Morayfield?

Pricing depends on size, excavation, access, finish, drainage and crossover scope. Send us the rough size and finish you want and we will give you a fixed written quote.

Can you replace an existing driveway?

Yes. We can remove the old concrete, prepare the base, correct levels where possible and pour a new driveway with the finish you choose.

How long before I can drive on it?

Light foot traffic is usually fine after 24 hours. We generally recommend waiting 7 days before vehicle traffic and 28 days for full cure strength.

Do you service nearby suburbs?

Yes. We regularly work across Caboolture, Morayfield, Caboolture South, Bellmere, Upper Caboolture, Burpengary and nearby Moreton Bay suburbs.
