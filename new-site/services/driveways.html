Our Process

How a Driveway Job Works

01

Free Quote

We come out to measure up and assess access, soil, drainage and your preferred finish. Written quote follows within 24 hours.

02

Excavation & Base Prep

We dig to the correct depth, compact the sub-grade and lay aggregate base — the most critical step most concreters rush.

03

Formwork & Steel

Formwork is set level and true. Steel mesh or reo bar is placed and lifted to the correct position in the slab.

04

Pour & Finish

Ready-mix concrete is poured, screeded, floated and finished to your chosen texture. Control joints are cut while the slab is green.

05

Cure & Seal

Curing compound or wet curing protects the concrete in its critical first week. Sealer applied once fully cured.
