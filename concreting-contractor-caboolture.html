Common questions

Questions we get asked before every job.

How much does a concrete driveway cost in Caboolture?

Pricing depends on the size, thickness, reinforcement requirements, finish type and site conditions — a broom finish driveway is priced differently from exposed aggregate or coloured concrete. We give a fixed written price after measuring up, not a loose per-square-metre estimate that changes at handover. Contact us for a same-day quote response.

What finish should I choose for my driveway?

Broom finish is the most popular choice for residential driveways in Caboolture — practical, hard-wearing and cost-effective. Exposed aggregate gives a premium decorative look with natural slip resistance. Coloured concrete adds a tonal finish to match your home's palette. We can show you samples on-site before you decide.

How long does concrete take to cure and when can I drive on it?

Light foot traffic is safe after 24–48 hours. We recommend keeping vehicles off for 7 days and the concrete reaches full design strength at 28 days. We give you a written handover sheet with the exact schedule for your pour so there is no guesswork.

Do you include steel reinforcement?

Yes — all our driveways and slabs include steel mesh or reo bar sized to the load requirements of the job. We do not cut corners on reinforcement because the slab is in the ground for decades and inadequate steel is the primary cause of premature cracking.

How long does it take from quote to pour?

We typically respond to quote requests same day and can visit within 48 hours. Scheduling from there depends on the time of year and job size — we will give you a realistic lead time when we come out to measure up. Smaller jobs such as paths and pads can often be scheduled within a few weeks.
